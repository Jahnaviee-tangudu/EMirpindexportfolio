About Me

During my internship at Edumoon, an innovative edtech startup, I gained invaluable experience in both technical and professional domains. Edumoon is dedicated to revolutionizing the educational landscape by providing interactive, technology-driven learning solutions that cater to diverse educational needs. Throughout my tenure, I honed my skills in web development, working with modern frameworks and tools to create user-friendly and responsive educational platforms.

I also enhanced my understanding of UX/UI design principles, ensuring that our applications were both visually appealing and easy to navigate. Additionally, I developed strong project management and collaboration skills, as I worked closely with a dynamic team of developers, designers, and educators to deliver high-quality digital learning experiences.
